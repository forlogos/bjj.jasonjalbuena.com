Wiki

my BJJ blog is proud to present it’s very own BJJ Techniques Wiki.

The wiki pages will allow you, citizens of the Internet, to edit pages within the wiki. For every post I have featuring a technique illustrated by my snowboard instructors, I’ll create a wiki page. This means that the text can be edited by anyone and that it can contain your thoughts, words, & ideas.

I’m hoping you will find this new feature useful and interesting to use. If you have any thoughts or ideas, please share them in the comments below. If you want a specific wiki page created, let me know in the comments too.

The following is a complete list of techniques included in the wiki:

  • Wiki: Double Underhook Guardpass To Armbar
  • Wiki: North-South Armbar and Some Options
  • Wiki: Open Guard to Single Leg Takedown
  • Wiki: Standing Outside Open Guard To Arm Bar
  • Wiki: Standing to open guard sweep to knee-on-belly to armbar
  • Wiki: Triangle and Options
