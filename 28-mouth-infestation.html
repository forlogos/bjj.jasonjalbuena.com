mouth infestation

Leave a comment  
In Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu, Injuries |

It seems that lately I always hurt my lip while training. Either someone does a bad choke on me, kicks me on the jaw, knees or elbows me. But I’m always going home with a bruised lip or some light bleeding.

The one from the last class was a big cut on my lip, which started bleeding a lot into my mouth. Anyway, a day later the cut started getting infected and hurting a lot, which is why I missed training last week. I can’t even put a mouthguard on. It’s getting better, but it still hurts to talk sometimes.

I’m going to start wearing a mouthguard EVERY time I roll from now on.
