welcome to myBJJblog.com

No Comments  

After several days of not putting out new posts, staying late at work, being up at night playing newly discovered computer games, and WordPress importing, tweaking, and general setting up – - I present to you my new BJJ blog, myBJJblog.com.

I will start putting all my new posts here and will stop putting up new BJJ posts at B Stuff Etc. If you’re a previous reader, I’m sorry that I’m unable to transition here as smoothly as Steve did when he moved his blog over to SteveBJJ.com.  All my BJJ posts, comments, and such from the old blog are all here.

If you have B Stuff Etc bookmarked or have any links, please kindly change it to myBJJblog.com. If you’re currently subscribed through my old Feedburner RSS feed, you won’t have to do a thing – that’s one smooth part of a transition I can offer. I have a new Feedburner RSS feed, http://feeds.feedburner.com/MyBjjBlog, for this new blog all set-up as well.

Thanks for reading…
