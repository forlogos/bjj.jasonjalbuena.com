UFC 94 thoughts

4 Comments  
In BJ Penn, MMA, thoughts, UFC |

The following are my thoughts and opinions, like it or not….

I dunno, I kinda went thru the whole thing not knowing what UFC 94 was titled.  Was there even a title?  Anyway, as with my other UFC thoughts I’ll avoid fight analysis etc like I’m sure is freely found online.

My sad observance is that there is a trend for less and less submissions at the UFC.  Why is TapOut still supporting the UFC?  I’m not a big UFC fan and I don’t follow it much, but I’ll watch if there are some fights I’m interested in (or if a friend is hosting at their place), so maybe I’m wrong.  But there weren’t any submissions at the last few UFCs I’ve seen.  The UFC is looking more like bad kickboxing than great MMA.

And BJ Penn, oh…. was he a disappointment.  He’s letting his talent go to waste.  I heard he went on a vacation from his training camp…what!?!?  While there are sure to be other issues (weight class, etc), it looks more like under-preparation and over confidence.  Like the proverb says, “Pride goes before destruction and a haughty spirit before a fall.”  I hope he can get and keep his act together since he was one of my favorite fighters.
