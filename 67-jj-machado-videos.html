JJ Machado videos

1 Comment  
In ADCC, Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu, JJ Machado, Renzo Gracie, Ricardo Arona, videos |

Just sharing some videos I enjoyed…


JJ Machado vs Ricardo Arona at ADCC. Even though he lost the fight, I was impressed with how JJ Machado rolled. He would make space to try different things, get several submission attempts, and play defense. Ricardo Arona looked like he was just bigger and stronger than JJ Machado but he was able to somewhat control the fight and defend submission attempts.


A short documentary I saw on JJ Machado.


Renzo Gracie sharing how he beat JJ Machado. He gives great insight on defending against a triangle.
